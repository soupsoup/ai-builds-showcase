📊 How Scoring Works

Contacts are categorized based on their title and company, then scored 0–100.


Category Who qualifies Base score
🎯 Hiring ManagerSenior title (VP/Director/Head) + domain match85
📋 RecruiterRecruiter, Talent, HR, People Ops title75
🏢 Target CompanyWorks at a company on your target list65
🤝 Domain PeerWorks in your domain, not senior55
🔗 Senior ConnectorSenior title in a target industry (not your domain)50
🏭 Industry ContactWorks in a target industry, no title/domain match30
💬 Weak TieNo match on domain, industry, or target company5

Bonuses: +15 target company (if not already Insider), +10 domain match bonus, +8 new connection, +6 dormant (>1yr), +up to 12 for 2nd-degree with multiple intro paths.
